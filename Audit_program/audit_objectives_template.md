# Audit Objectives Template

## Primary Objectives
- Evaluate effectiveness of technical and administrative controls.
- Verify compliance with internal policies and regulatory requirements.
- Identify weaknesses that may expose the organization to security risk.

## Secondary Objectives
- Validate alignment with ISO 27001:2022 Annex A / COBIT / NIST.
- Assess whether monitoring and reporting mechanisms are effective.
- Determine maturity of processes and identify improvements.
