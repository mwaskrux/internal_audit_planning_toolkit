# Annual Audit Plan

## 1. Audit Objectives
To provide independent assurance on IT governance, cybersecurity controls, operational efficiency, and compliance with relevant standards.

## 2. Planned Audits
| Audit Area               | Frequency | Risk Rating | Owner | Quarter |
|-------------------------|-----------|-------------|-------|---------|
| Access Control          | Annual    | High        | ICT   | Q1      |
| Network Security        | Annual    | High        | ICT   | Q2      |
| Backup & DR             | Annual    | Medium      | ICT   | Q3      |
| Change Management       | Annual    | Medium      | ICT   | Q4      |

## 3. Resource Requirements
- Lead Auditor (1)
- Technical SME (1)
- ICT Officer support (as needed)

## 4. Alignment
- ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Annex A  
- COBIT 2019 Governance Objectives  
- Organizational risk register
