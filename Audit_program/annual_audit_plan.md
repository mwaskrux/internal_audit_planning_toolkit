# Annual IT Internal Audit Plan

## 1. Overview
This plan defines all scheduled IT and cybersecurity audits for the organization within the financial year. The plan is risk-based and aligns to ISO/IEC 27001:2022, COBIT 2019, and the enterprise risk register.

## 2. Objectives
- Provide independent assurance on the effectiveness of IT controls.
- Assess compliance with internal policies and regulatory requirements.
- Evaluate cybersecurity readiness across critical domains.
- Support executive leadership in risk-informed decision making.

## 3. Planned Audits
| Audit Area               | Risk Rating | Frequency | Quarter | Audit Type | Control Framework |
|--------------------------|-------------|-----------|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| Access Control           | High        | Annual    | Q1      | Operational | ISO 27001 A.5, A.6 |
| Network Security         | High        | Annual    | Q2      | Technical   | ISO 27001 A.8 |
| Backup & Disaster Recovery | Medium    | Annual    | Q3      | Resilience  | ISO 27001 A.5, A.8 |
| Change Management        | Medium      | Annual    | Q4      | Operational | COBIT BAI06 |
| Vendor IT Risk           | Medium      | Bi-Annual | Q2/Q4   | Compliance  | ISO 27001 A.15 |

## 4. Resource Requirements
- Lead Auditor (1)
- Technical SME (1)
- ICT Support Staff (as needed)

## 5. Reporting
Quarterly audit reports submitted to:
- CIO
- Risk & Compliance Department
- Audit Committee

## 6. Alignment & Dependencies
- Corporate risk register
- Information security strategy
- Incident history from SOC/IT
